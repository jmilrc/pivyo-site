Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a focus session?

Tap the play button on the main timer screen. You can choose between Focus, Short Break, or Long Break sessions. Customize durations in Settings.

What is a streak?

A streak counts consecutive days where you've completed at least one focus session. Build your streak to develop consistent focus habits!

How do streak freezes work?

Streak freezes protect your streak on days you can't focus (weekends, holidays, etc.). Premium users get unlimited streak freezes.

Can I use Pivyo offline?

Yes! Pivyo works completely offline. All your data is stored on your device.

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to Settings > Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your device to manage or cancel.

Is my data synced across devices?

If you enable iCloud sync in Settings, your data will sync across all devices signed into the same iCloud account.
