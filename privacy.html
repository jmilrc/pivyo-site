Pivyo Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 7, 2026

Pivyo ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use Pivyo.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

Pivyo is designed with privacy as a core principle. All your data stays on your device:

  • Focus session history
  • Goals and streaks
  • Achievements and progress
  • App preferences and settings
  • Session notes and labels

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's secure data storage. We do not have access to your data, and it is never transmitted to our servers.

If you enable iCloud sync (optional), your data is synced through your personal iCloud account, which is governed by Apple's privacy policy.

Third-Party Services

Pivyo may integrate with the following Apple services at your discretion:

  • Apple Health (HealthKit) - If enabled, Pivyo can write mindful minutes to Apple Health. This data stays within Apple's Health ecosystem.
  • Calendar (EventKit) - If enabled, Pivyo can read your calendar to suggest optimal focus times. We do not store or transmit calendar data.
  • StoreKit - For processing in-app purchases. Transactions are handled securely by Apple.

Analytics and Tracking

Pivyo does not include any analytics SDKs, tracking pixels, or advertising frameworks. We do not track your usage or behavior.

In-App Purchases

Premium subscriptions and purchases are processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

Children's Privacy

Pivyo does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect your data, there's nothing to request, modify, or delete from our end. You can delete all app data by uninstalling Pivyo from your device.
